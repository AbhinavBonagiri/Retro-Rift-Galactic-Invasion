# Retro-Rift-Galactic-Invasion
Retro-styled space shooter in which the player controls a spaceship battling waves of evil aliens from outer space. Follows an arcade format, 
requiring players to dodge enemy fire while eliminating them using power-ups and various weapons.

## Features
- Collision Detection
  - A hitbox-based detection system when a player’s projectile 
collides with an enemy, or likewise, when an enemy's projectile collides with the 
player. When the collision occurs, the affected player or enemy will take damage 
to their health. If the health goes negative, the player will die and lose the game, 
or the enemy will disappear from the game. 
- Basic Enemy and Player Shooting Mechanics
  - The player can shoot projectiles based on mouse clicks and the position of the spaceship. Enemies will 
shoot projectiles in their predefined movement and will fire at a predefined rate. 
- Player Status UI
  - A score counter and health bar is shown to the player for the duration of the game.
- Power-ups
  - Power-ups spawn randomly for the  player to collect. When collected, the power-ups will temporarily boost the player, 
giving the player a shield, increasing the player's attack speed, and different 
attack patterns that the enemies can do, such as lasers. 
 - Scoring System and Player Stats 
   - During the game, the player will increase their high score by killing enemies. On the homepage and menu selection, there 
will be an option to view player stats. 
- Thematic Music and SFX
  - The game will feature retro-inspired background 
music related to the wave and battlefield in the background they are in. For SFX, 
there will be sound effects for explosions, player shooting, and enemy shooting.
